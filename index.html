Why it's different

Sugar slows hydration down. Osmo doesn't use it.

Sugar makes a drink hypertonic — your stomach empties slower and water reaches your cells later. Osmo is hypotonic and zero-added-sugar, so fluid moves fast.

⚡

Faster in, no slump

No added sugar keeps Osmo hypotonic — water and minerals move into your cells quicker. No sugar rush, no crash, no 4pm slump.

🧬

Magnesium isn't optional

Your cellular pump needs all three: sodium, potassium and magnesium. Most hydration options drop the third. Osmo delivers the complete triad your cells actually ask for.
